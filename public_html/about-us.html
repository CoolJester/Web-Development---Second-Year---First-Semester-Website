History

The history of BNL Entertainment is not a long one. The orgranisation was founded in 2019 by two young individuals who had a burning passion for photography. At the time this small group didn't have the equipment to take professional pictures but as time went on they were able to raise some capital from taking pictures at birthday parties and taking pictures of people they met using just their mobile devices. Then after they registered the business at the beginning of 2020. They grew more and more as time passed during that year and they were also able to gain trust of other organisation and making valuable contacts. Their reputation was self-made and they have always been able to make ends meet.

Mission

BNL Entertainment aims to provide affordable services and products to the public, we ensure that the end product is of a high quality always. Media files such as photos and videos are being copywrited these days. This prohibits people from using this content freely but we at the BNL Entertainment give people a chance to make their own content at affordable prices.

Vision

We soon seek to be internationally recongnized in most countries in the world. We don't aim to be known only in South Africa, if we dream small then we won't be able to expand our business to the lengths we seek.
